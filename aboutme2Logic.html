In this town, kids can say only truths or only lies. Alice, Bob, Clara, David, and Ethan are having conversations. There are 3 truthtellers and 2 liars. Who's who?

Alice: "Bob is lying."
Bob: "Alice is lying."
Clara: "Both Alice and Bob are lying."
David: "Clara is lying."
Ethan: "Alice and David are telling the truth."

Explanation

Alice and Bob are saying each other is a liar. So Alice and Bob cannot be both true or both false. That leads Clara a liar. David is a truthteller because Clara is a liar. We don't know yet whether Ethan is a liar at all. So let's check what is his identity.

If he is telling the truth, then Alice is a truthteller and Bob is a liar. And there must be 3 truthtellers(Alice, David, and Ethan) and 2 liars(Bob and Clara).

If he were a liar, then Bob is telling the truth and there would be 2 truthtellers(Bob and David) and 3 liars(Alice, Clara, Ethan).

So Alice, David, and Ethan are the truthtellers and Bob and Clara are the liars.
